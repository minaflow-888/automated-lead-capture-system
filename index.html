THE CONTEXT

What problem needed to be solved?

When lead handling depends on manual steps, the business loses speed exactly where speed matters most. Even small delays create friction for the team and increase the chance of inconsistent records.

The problem

  • Leads were not moving through the process fast enough
  • Manual handling created repetitive admin work
  • Important details could become scattered between tools
  • The team lacked one clean, reliable lead record

The goal

  • Capture every lead through one clear entry point
  • Route submissions automatically without manual copying
  • Store the data in a structured format
  • Notify the team immediately so follow-up can start faster

THE SOLUTION

A connected workflow designed around business outcomes

Instead of treating the process as a set of disconnected tools, I designed it as one system with a clear flow: collect the lead, process the data, store it cleanly, and notify the team right away.

Capture

A Webflow form collects lead information through a simple and consistent front-end experience.

Route

Make handles the submission logic automatically, removing unnecessary manual steps.

Store

Google Sheets acts as a structured source of truth for incoming lead data.

Notify

Slack alerts give the team immediate visibility so follow-up can happen without delay.
