THE PROBLEM

Manual workflows create friction exactly where speed matters most

Even a basic lead process becomes fragile when information has to be copied, checked, or forwarded by hand. The business was losing response time and internal trust.

What was going wrong

  • ⏱️ Leads not moving fast enough through the funnel
  • 🔄 Repetitive manual copy/paste between tools
  • 📌 Scattered lead details → lost context
  • 👀 No single source of truth for incoming leads

What the workflow needed to do

  • ✅ One clear entry point for every lead
  • ⚙️ Automatic routing – zero manual handoff
  • 🗂️ Structured, consistent data storage
  • 🔔 Instant team alert to act while it’s fresh
THE SOLUTION

A connected no‑code workflow designed for clarity & reliability

One unified system: capture → process → store → notify. No more manual glue.

Capture

Webflow form as a clean, consistent entry point for every inbound lead.

Route

Make.com handles logic automatically, removing manual handoff & human error.

Store

Google Sheets logs each submission → structured, auditable source of truth.

Notify

Slack alert triggers instantly, helping teams follow up within minutes.

VISUAL WORKFLOW

How the process moves from submission to team notification

End-to-end automation in action: each step eliminates manual work and adds visibility.

01

Lead capture begins on the website

A native Webflow form creates the only entry point, ensuring zero lost leads and validation.

02

Automation logic routes the data

Make receives the webhook, processes fields, enriches if needed — consistent and auditable.

03

Structured storage in Google Sheets

Every submission is appended with timestamp + lead details, giving operations a clean database.

04

Real‑time Slack notification

A formatted message hits the designated channel → team can act in seconds, not hours.
