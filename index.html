THE PROBLEM

Manual workflows create friction where speed matters most

When lead handling depends on manual steps, businesses lose time exactly when they should be moving fast. Even a basic process becomes fragile when information has to be copied, checked, or forwarded by hand.

What was going wrong

  • Leads were not moving through the process quickly enough
  • Manual handoff created repetitive low-value admin work
  • Important details could become scattered between tools
  • The team lacked one clean real-time view of incoming leads

What the workflow needed to do

  • Capture every lead through one clear entry point
  • Route the submission automatically without manual copying
  • Store the data in a structured and consistent format
  • Alert the team immediately so follow-up can start faster

THE SOLUTION

A connected no-code workflow designed for clarity and reliability

Instead of treating the process as separate tools, I designed it as one connected system: capture the lead, process the data, store it cleanly, and notify the team instantly.

Capture

A Webflow form creates a clean, consistent entry point for every incoming lead.

Route

Make handles the workflow logic automatically, removing manual handoff from the process.

Store

Google Sheets keeps submissions in one structured record for visibility and review.

Notify

Slack alerts give the team immediate awareness, so no lead sits unnoticed.
